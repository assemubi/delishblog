Italian Pasta Bolognese

Pasta Bolognese is a rich and hearty Italian pasta dish made with ground meat, tomatoes, and aromatic vegetables. Itâ€™s a perfect comfort food and a favorite for many.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, finely chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb pasta (tagliatelle or fettuccine)
  • Fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and pork, cooking until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onions, carrots, celery, and garlic. Cook until softened.
  3. Return the meat to the pot and pour in the red wine. Simmer until the wine has mostly evaporated.
  4. Add the beef broth, crushed tomatoes, and tomato paste. Stir to combine.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for at least 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in the whole milk and cook for another 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and add to the Bolognese sauce, tossing to combine.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
