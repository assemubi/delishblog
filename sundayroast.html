Sunday Roast Chicken

Sunday Roast Chicken is a traditional British meal that's perfect for family gatherings. It features a whole roasted chicken served with potatoes, vegetables, and gravy.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4-5 lbs)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 lemons, quartered
  • 4 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 lbs potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 lb carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 lb parsnips, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup chicken broth

  2. Rinse the chicken and pat it dry with paper towels. Season the cavity with salt and pepper.
  3. Stuff the cavity with minced garlic, lemon quarters, rosemary, and thyme.
  4. Rub the outside of the chicken with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Place the chicken in a roasting pan and surround it with the chopped potatoes, carrots, and parsnips.
  6. Pour the chicken broth into the pan.
  7. Roast in the preheated oven for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (75째C).
  8. Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving. Serve with the roasted vegetables and gravy.
