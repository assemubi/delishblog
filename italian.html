Experience the rich flavors of Italy with dishes that are beloved around the world. Italian cuisine is known for its use of fresh ingredients, simple preparations, and bold flavors.

Popular Dishes

  • Pizza: A world-famous open pie baked with tomato sauce, cheese and a variety of optional ingredients. Among the classic varieties of Italian pizza are Margherita (with products in the colours of the Italian flag: mozzarella - white, tomato sauce - red, basil - green), Capricciosa, Diabola, Quattro formaggi ("Four Cheeses") and Quattro stagioni ("Four Seasons")
  • Margherita Pizza: A classic pizza topped with tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil.
  • Risotto alla Milanese: A dish of rice (high starch varieties are used) in broth/water. Among the classic risottos is risotto alla milanese with onions and saffron.
