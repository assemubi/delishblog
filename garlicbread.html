Garlic Bread

Garlic bread is a popular and delicious accompaniment to many meals, particularly Italian dishes like pasta and pizza. It combines the rich flavors of garlic and butter with a crispy bread base, creating a savory and irresistible treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of French bread or Italian bread
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the loaf of bread in half horizontally.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and a pinch of salt. Mix until well combined.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut sides of the bread.
  5. If desired, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the buttered bread for an extra layer of flavor.
  6. Place the bread halves on a baking sheet, cut side up.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the bread is crispy and the butter has melted.
  8. For a crispier texture, you can broil the garlic bread for an additional 1-2 minutes, keeping a close eye to prevent burning.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes. Slice into individual pieces and serve warm.

Additional Tips

  • For a different twist, try adding a bit of mozzarella cheese on top before baking for a cheesy garlic bread.
  • Feel free to experiment with herbs like basil or oregano to add more flavor.
  • If you prefer a more subtle garlic flavor, roast the garlic cloves before mixing them with the butter.
